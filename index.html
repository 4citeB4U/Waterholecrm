About Water Hole

Water Hole is an initiative created to transform how community organizations work together in Milwaukee. We recognized that organizations addressing interconnected community challenges often operate in isolation, creating gaps and inefficiencies in service delivery.

Our Connect CRM system provides the digital infrastructure that enables organizations from different sectors to discover each other, form partnerships, coordinate services, and measure collective impact. By connecting organizations across traditional boundaries, we're building a more collaborative and effective community service ecosystem.

The platform currently connects over 100 organizations across 20+ service categories, including youth development, housing, food security, workforce development, education, and health services.

The Challenge We Address

Community organizations often work in isolation despite serving the same populations and addressing interconnected issues.

Disconnected Services

Organizations providing complementary services often don't know about each other or lack formal channels for collaboration, creating gaps and duplications in service.

Resource Inefficiency

Limited community resources are often stretched thin when organizations cannot easily share expertise, facilities, funding opportunities, and best practices.

Fragmented Client Journeys

Community members must navigate complex, disconnected systems to access the full range of services they need, creating barriers to support.

How Connect CRM Works

1. Organization Profiles

Each organization in the system has a comprehensive profile that includes:

  • Organization name, category, and contact information
  • Detailed description of services provided
  • Physical location and service area
  • Operating hours and current status
  • Staff members and key contacts

2. Discover Organizations

Users can easily find potential partners through multiple discovery methods:

  • Search by organization name, service type, or location
  • Filter by category, status, or services offered
  • Browse the visual organization directory
  • View recommended organizations based on complementary services
  • Explore existing organization networks

3. Create Connections

The system facilitates intentional relationship-building between organizations:

  • Request links with individual organizations
  • Create link groups with multiple organizations
  • Accept or decline incoming link requests
  • Visually manage organization connections
  • Document partnership details and agreements

4. Collaborate & Track Impact

Connected organizations can work together more effectively:

  • Create and manage joint projects
  • Track project milestones and progress
  • Share notes and documentation
  • Measure collective outcomes and impact
  • Visualize network relationships and referral pathways
